PyDiff holds regular informal meetups in Cardiff for anyone who uses or wants to learn about Python. Everyone is welcome, from experienced programmers to new learners, wherever their interest lies. The usual format is a series of lightning talks (everyone is welcome to talk) about Python related topics but longer form talks also take place.
